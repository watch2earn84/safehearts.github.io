WhatsCleaner – WhatsApp Media Cleaner App

Is your phone running out of space?

WhatsCleaner is the ultimate tool to manage and clean media files you receive through WhatsApp and WhatsApp Business. Videos, images, and audio can take up gigabytes of storage without you realizing it. WhatsCleaner helps you find and remove unnecessary files safely and efficiently.

Key Features

  • Organized Library: Browse your WhatsApp Images, Videos, and Audio files in a clean, organized interface.
  • One-Tap Clean: Select unwanted files and delete them instantly to recover storage space.
  • Fast and Smooth: Efficient thumbnail loading using Glide technology for smooth scrolling.
  • Privacy First: All file management happens locally on your device. WhatsCleaner never uploads your files or personal data to servers.
  • Modern Design: Built with Material Design 3 for an intuitive and beautiful user experience.

How WhatsCleaner Helps

WhatsCleaner minimizes the risk of accidental deletion of important media by letting you organize, preview, and manage files. Whether you want to clear space or just keep your media organized, WhatsCleaner provides a simple and safe solution.
