WhatsClean – Smart WhatsApp Cleaner App for Android

Clean WhatsApp images, videos, audio, and documents to free storage safely.

WhatsClean is a smart and lightweight WhatsApp cleaner app for Android designed to help users free up storage space by identifying unnecessary WhatsApp images, videos, audio files, documents, and forwarded media. It supports both WhatsApp and WhatsApp Business and works completely offline.

Key Features of WhatsClean

  • Scan WhatsApp images, videos, audio, GIFs, and documents
  • Identify large, duplicate, and forwarded media
  • Preview files before deleting
  • Sort files by size, date, and type
  • Supports WhatsApp & WhatsApp Business
  • 100% offline processing for privacy

How WhatsClean Works

WhatsClean scans WhatsApp media folders on your device and organizes files into categories. You can preview files and safely delete only what you don’t need. Chats and messages are never accessed or modified.

Why Choose WhatsClean?

WhatsApp automatically downloads large amounts of media, which can quickly fill your phone storage. WhatsCleaner gives you full control over WhatsApp storage without using cloud servers or collecting personal data.

Privacy & Data Safety

  • No login or account required
  • No data collection or tracking
  • No cloud uploads
  • All processing happens locally on your device
