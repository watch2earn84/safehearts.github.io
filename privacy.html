Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 07, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how WhatsCleaner (“the App”) handles user data. WhatsCleaner is a utility app designed to help users manage and clean WhatsApp and WhatsApp Business media files stored locally on their device.

1. Data Collection and Usage

Personal Data:
WhatsCleaner does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information such as names, phone numbers, email addresses, or contacts.

Media Files:
The App accesses WhatsApp and WhatsApp Business media files only after explicit user permission is granted through Android’s Storage Access Framework (SAF). Access is limited strictly to folders selected by the user. All processing occurs locally on the device, and media files are never uploaded or shared.

2. Permissions

  • Folder Access (Storage Access Framework): Used to view, manage, and delete media files only within user-selected WhatsApp and WhatsApp Business folders.
  • Media Permissions (READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_MEDIA_VIDEO): Used to display previews and thumbnails of selected media files.
  • Internet: Used only for essential app functionality such as loading libraries or non-personal content.

3. Third-Party Services

WhatsCleaner may use Google Play Services, which operate under their own privacy policies.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

WhatsCleaner does not store user data on external servers. Any media deletion is performed only upon user action and permanently removes the file from the device.

5. Children’s Privacy

WhatsCleaner is not intended for children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.
